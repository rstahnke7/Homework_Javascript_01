Bill Watrous – Master of the Trombone

About Bill Watrous

William Russell Watrous III (1939–2018) was a world-renowned jazz trombonist known for his smooth tone and remarkable technique. His rendition of “A Time for Love” remains one of the most beautiful ballads in jazz history.

Watrous played with legends like Count Basie and Quincy Jones, and led acclaimed ensembles such as the Manhattan Wildlife Refuge.
